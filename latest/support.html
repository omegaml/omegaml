Support¶

Community Edition¶

The community edition is a free and open-core version of omega-ml. It is designed for individuals and small teams who want to use omega-ml for their projects. The community edition includes all the core features of omega-ml, including the ability to create and manage data products, run experiments, and deploy models.

Contributions are welcome at https://github.com/omegaml/omegaml

Documentation https://omegaml.github.io/omegaml/

Commercial Edition and Support¶

omega-ml Commercial Edition provides security on every level and is ready made for Kubernetes deployment. It is licensed separately for deployment on on-premise, private or hybrid cloud. Get in touch at https://omegaml.io
